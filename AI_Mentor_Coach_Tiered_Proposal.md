## Project Overview

Transform a proprietary Mental Toughness & Psychological Flexibility playbook into an interactive AI Mentor Coach that:

- Guides users through reflection and growth
- Maintains strict adherence to the playbook's philosophy

#### What You Get
A functional AI chatbot that knows your playbook and can answer user questions accurately.

- **Basic RAG Implementation**: Playbook content ingested into AI knowledge base
- **Simple Q&A**: Users can ask questions and get answers grounded in your material
- **Web Chat Widget**: Embeddable on one website (your LMS or main site)
- **Basic Persona**: Tone and style aligned with your methodology
- **Timeline**: 2 weeks

#### What the Chatbot Can Do
✓ Answer direct questions about concepts in the playbook
✓ Reference specific frameworks and techniques

✗ No structured journeys or reflection prompts

#### Best For
Organizations wanting to make their playbook more accessible and searchable, but not requiring deep coaching capabilities.

- Basic setup guide provided
